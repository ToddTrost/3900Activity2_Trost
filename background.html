My Background


SSgt Todd A. Trost, "General Manager of the Universe"


(Image: man furthest to the right) I've been active duty in the United States Air Force for almost a decade. Of that decade, 5 years have been spent doing meteorology in the form of airfield forecasting, aviation hazard briefings, and upper-low atmospheric analysis. The last 5 years have been entirely focused on space weather. Within that timeframe, I've completed dozens of space weather and Air Force space related courses, programs, and conferences. Accomplishing thousands of hours of OJT has led to my long stint as a team lead, shift supervisor, leading training for young Airmen, and revising/built hundreds of OJT training requirements alongside developing new positional certification checklists.


Fun Fact: The "General Manager of the Universe" title appended to me is a desk plate we display on our lead position's desk. Anyone who reaches that point gets to throw the title around for visitors we get.
